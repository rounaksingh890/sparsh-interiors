The Studio

Homes designed around the people who live in them.

Sparsh Interiors is a women-owned studio in Sukhdev Nagar, Panipat, led by Sparsh Khanna. The work is warm and personal: spaces shaped around how a family actually lives, styled to feel like a calm, luxurious version of home.

Every project is walked through from the first idea to the finished room, with 2D planning and 3D visualisation so you see it before it's built, and designs tailored to your taste and your budget. Clients keep it simple in their reviews: a spotless 5.0-star rating.
